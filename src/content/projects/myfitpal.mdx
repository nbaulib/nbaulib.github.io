## Context
My friends and I wanted something to do over winter break. We all dipped our toes into food tracking but nothing ever stuck.

After some talking, we decided to redesign MyFitnessPal (MFP), a widely used nutrition app, to be more mindful of users with disordered eating habits.
{/* MyFitnessPal (MFP) is a widely used nutrition and fitness app with 200M+ users globally. While effective for weight and fitness tracking, its heavy emphasis on calories and weight can negatively impact users with disordered eating habits. */}

{/* ## Research 
Weight management apps often [lack behavioral and evidence-based strategies](https://www.ajpmonline.org/article/S0749-3797(13)00431-5/abstract), prioritizing functionality over user well-being. 

As a result, MFP can support goal-setting but may [reinforce unhealthy behaviors for users with poor body image or obsessive tendencies](https://www.sciencedirect.com/science/article/abs/pii/S1740144521001194?via%3Dihub). */}

## Case Study

{/* ## Problem Statement 
How might we make MFP more flexible and supportive (*friendlier*) for users with disordered eating habits?

## User Insight 
After our interviews, we created Lakpa Sherpa, a 20-year-old student recovering from anorexia.

<ImageBlock
  images={[
    { src: lakpa, alt: "user persona" },
  ]}
/> */}

{/* These are her core needs:
- Reduce focus on calories and weight  
- Support strength-based nutrition (macros)  
- Simple food tracking  
- Encourage consistency over perfection   */}

{/* ## Pain Points
- Overwhelming, calorie-centric interface  
- Rigid meal categories  
- Lack of positive, adaptive feedback  
- Complex food entry workflows   */}

{/* ## Solutions

- **Customizable Dashboard**  
  Users can hide calorie/weight tracking and prioritize macros. A redesigned Weekly Digest highlights consistency and positive progress.

- **Encouraging Feedback System**  
  Shifts from rigid goal completion to supportive messaging focused on habit-building.

- **Flexible Food Logging**  
  Replaces strict meal categories with “Food Entries,” supporting intuitive eating patterns.

- **Collapsible Meal Entries**  
  Simplifies multi-ingredient meals, reducing cognitive overload.

- **Improved Search & Item View**  
  Prioritizes protein/macros while making calorie details optional, reducing triggering exposure.

- **Educational Content Integration**  
  Surfaces relevant, engaging nutrition content to empower users. */}
